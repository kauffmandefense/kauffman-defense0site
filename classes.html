Michigan Concealed Pistol License (CPL) Course

Our Michigan Concealed Pistol License (CPL) Course, **certified by the Michigan Coalition for Responsible Gun Owners (MCRGO)**, is designed to meet all state requirements for obtaining your CPL. This **comprehensive training** covers essential topics to ensure you understand the **responsibilities of carrying a concealed firearm** while equipping you with the **necessary skills for safe and effective firearm use**.

What You’ll Learn

  • Michigan Firearms Laws – Understand legal responsibilities, use-of-force laws, and firearm transport regulations.
  • Personal Safety and Conflict Avoidance – Learn how to recognize threats, de-escalate conflicts, and stay safe.
  • Firearm Safety and Handling – Hands-on training in firearm operation, safe handling, and proper storage.
  • Fundamentals of Marksmanship – Improve stance, grip, sight alignment, trigger control, and recoil management.
  • Live-Fire Training – Participate in a **supervised shooting exercise** to demonstrate safe handling and competency.

Course Details

Location: Gaylord, Michigan

Duration: 8 Hours (Classroom and Range Time)

Live Fire Requirement: Yes (Required by Michigan law)

Certification: Official MCRGO CPL Training Certificate upon successful completion.

This class is perfect for **first-time CPL applicants** and those wanting to **refresh their knowledge**. Whether you are **new to firearms** or an **experienced shooter**, our **expert instructors** will ensure you leave **confident and prepared**.
